About Us

NSIS’s History

Nile Sons International School is an American and British accredited school located in the 6th District of Obour City. NSIS opened its doors to students in the 2016/2017 academic year, and offers state of the art facilities, highly qualified staff, and the newest and most innovative international curricula. NSIS has an after-school Sports Academy, and is also an ACT Testing Center.
NSIS also offers after-school ACT Preparation Courses for English and Math.

NSIS School Values

• Cooperation: NSIS encourages and fosters a teamwork environment to achieve common goals.
• Community Service: NSIS values community participation and involvement for the betterment of the nation.
• Diversity: NSIS values individual differences and multicultural backgrounds.
• Leadership: NSIS is committed to quality leadership standards and performance to create a professional atmosphere.

NSIS Vision

Our vision is to become a landmark for international education in Egypt, whose graduates through knowledge, skills and values will be prepared to excel in a complex, interconnected and ever-changing world.

NSIS Mission

Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills and values that will enable them to serve their community and succeed in higher education by providing quality international curricula that are based on 21st Century skills.
