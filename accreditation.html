Accreditations/Partnerships

Nile Sons International School is fully accredited for both American Diploma and British Certificate, and is an ACT Testing Center.

AIAA

Founded in 1992 American Educational Consulting Associates, Ltd. or AECA, has been providing educational services to schools for over 25 years. AECA instituted AIAA to provide accreditation for schools outside of the United States that offer an international program or an American style curriculum. You can read more about AIAA’s vision, mission, beliefs, their awesome leadership, their accreditation standards, and their policies by going to aiaasc.com/about.

What are ACT Exams and ACT Aspire?

The ACT (American College Testing) is a standardized test used for college admissions in the United States as well as more than 225 universities outside of the U.S. It was first introduced in November 1959 as a competitor to the Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT). The ACT test covers four academic skill areas: English, mathematics, reading, and science reasoning. It also offers an optional direct writing test. Nile Sons International School is an ACT Testing Center.
ACT Aspire is an external assessment which is designed to measure how well students are understanding topics, concepts and skills required to succeed under the Common Core standards and focus on identifying what students should know at the end of each grade level All international schools outside of the United States are required to have external assessments to fulfill our accreditation requirements. The NSIS Administration has chosen ACT Aspire for our school’s external assessment.
If you would like to learn more about ACT and ACT Aspire, you can go to act.org.

Cambridge

Cambridge Assessment International Education is the world’s largest provider of international education programmes and qualifications for 5 to 19 year olds. Success in Cambridge qualifications often gives students admission to the world’s best universities – in the US, the UK, Australia, Canada, Germany and beyond.
Cambridge qualifications are accepted and valued by universities around the world, including MIT, Harvard and Cambridge.
They are recognized as qualifications that prepare and equip students with the skills, they need to succeed both at university and beyond. Universities tell us that they value the independent research and critical thinking skills, as well as the deep subject knowledge that our qualifications bring.
It’s what you’d expect from an organization that is part of the world-leading University of Cambridge. If you would like more information about Cambridge Assessment International Education, you can go to www.cambridgeinternational.org .

Pearson Edexcel International

Pearson Edexcel International GCSEs are globally recognized qualifications with academic content and assessment designed specifically for international learners. … It delivers a consistent learning journey for students and teachers, with world class support services, everywhere in the world.
Pearson Edexcel International AS/A Levels (IAL) are globally recognised qualifications which open doors to top universities worldwide. Available for 21 subjects, they have a modular structure, yet remain comparable to GCE AS/A levels, as confirmed by NARIC, the national agency responsible for providing information and expert opinion on qualifications and skills worldwide.
If you would like to know more about Pearson qualifications, go to qualifications.pearson.com.

Oxford

Oxford AQA is a fresh new international qualifications provider – but with a solid reputation of excellence.
They are a partnership between Oxford University Press, a department of the University of Oxford, and AQA, the UK’s largest provider of GCSEs and A-levels.
Their International GCSEs and A-levels are based on the most popular AQA qualifications taken in the UK. They have been revised to meet the needs of students studying in the Middle East, South East Asia and China. If you would like to know more about Oxford International AQA Examinations, you may go to www.oxfordaqaexams.org.uk.
